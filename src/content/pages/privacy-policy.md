PickyFox (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the information you may share while using this website. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected, how it may be used, and your options regarding your data.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

When you visit PickyFox, we may collect certain information automatically, including:

- Your device type, browser, operating system, and general location.
- Pages you visit, time spent on the site, and how you interact with content.
- Referral sources (how you arrived at our website).

We may also collect information you choose to provide voluntarily, such as when you contact us through a form.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

- Understand and improve website performance and user experience.
- Analyze traffic and trends using **third-party analytics providers**.
- Provide, maintain, and improve our content and services.
- Monitor site usage for security and fraud prevention.

---

## 3. Third-Party Services & Data Sharing

To operate effectively, PickyFox relies on **external service providers** such as analytics platforms and affiliate partners. These third parties may collect or process data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Examples include (but are not limited to):

- Website analytics tools to understand traffic and performance.
- Affiliate marketing programs, either directly or through partner websites, which may track clicks and purchases.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. However, using affiliate links may involve **cookies and tracking technologies** that attribute purchases to our website.

---

## 4. Cookies & Tracking

PickyFox may use cookies, pixels, and similar technologies to:

- Improve site functionality.
- Remember user preferences.
- Track affiliate links and measure marketing performance.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features of the site may not function properly without them.

---

## 5. External Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (including affiliate links). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites. Please review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

---

## 6. Your Choices

You can:

- Adjust browser settings to limit cookies.
- Opt out of certain analytics and ad tracking tools (check your browser or device privacy settings).
- Contact us directly if you have concerns about how your data is handled.
